About Me:

My name is Colin I am 25 years old and I was born and raised in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. I am currently studying to become a software developer at Bow Valley College in their Software Development Diploma program. I first got into the idea of doing software development back around middle school, but my mind kept changing as to what I wanted to do for a career path. Over the years I took many different jobs in a variety of fields and even set out into the trades for a while working as a steamfitter. One day I decided that I wanted to follow my passion of coding and working with computers and set out to figure out how I was going to make it happen. I am currently in my second year at Bow Valley and couldn't be happier with my choice to pursue becoming a computer programmer.
