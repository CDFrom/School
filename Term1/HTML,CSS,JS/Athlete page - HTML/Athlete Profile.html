Kyle Lowry:

Kyle Terrel Lowry is a professional basketball player in the NBA (National Basketball Association). He is currently 35 years old and was born on Marth 25 1986 in Philadelphia Pennsylvania. Kyle is 6 feet tall weighing 196 lbs. and has been playing professional basketball since 2006. He plays the role of point guard and won an NBA championship back in 2019 with the Toronto Raptors.

Prior to his time in the professional scene, Lowry had been playing college basketball at Villanova University in Pennsylvania for the Villanova Wildcats. He started off playing for the Grizzlies in 2006 and continued with them up until 2009 when he was traded to the Houston Rockets. In his last year of playing for the Toronto Raptors Kyle recorded his 4000th assist with the team being one of only 3 players to have over 4000 on their current team. Just shy of 2 weeks after this event he reached yet another milestone achieving 10,000 points with the team again being only 1 of 3 to achieve this while on the Toronto Raptors. After 9 years of playing for the Toronto Raptors, reaching these milestones and acquiring the first ever NBA championship for the team in its franchise history, Lowry would be traded off to Miami. On August 6 2021 Kyle was traded off to the Miami Heat.

Kyle Terrel Lowry has been playing basketball professionally for 15 years and his time with the Toronto Raptors definitely gave him a well-earned reputation. He led the team to many a victory as well as many franchise firsts. He will be known and remembered by the franchise, fans and teammates alike for years to come.
